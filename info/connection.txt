## Trade in services



This dataset is displaying OECD countries and the aim is to collect and disseminate
balance of payments data on international trade in services between UK, USA, Canada and
Australie with rest of OECD countries. To the extent that countries report them, data are also broken
down by type of service according to the EBOPS 2010 classification.
